\section{Definitions}

The fourier transform of an integrable function $f \colon \mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}$ is defined as 
\begin{equation}
	\hat{f}(\xi) = \mathcal{F}(f)(\xi) \coloneqq \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} f(x) e^{-2 \pi \mathrm{i} x \cdot \xi} \, \mathrm{d}x.
\end{equation}
